Project Description

In this project, I developed the main character, implementing animations provided by the artist and designing a fluid combo system. I also created a scoring system that rewards players for defeating enemies and a combo rating system ranging from C (lowest) to S (highest), encouraging skillful play.

The game was designed around the Game Jam's theme: "Health has to be sacrificed." To align with this, we introduced a unique mechanic centered around a special object: the Rose. The protagonist wields a thorned rose as a weapon, suffering self-inflicted damage with each attack. However, successfully hitting an enemy restores lost health, creating a dynamic risk-reward system that challenges players to balance aggression and survival.
