Project Description

This project was developed as part of a university assignment and focuses on tank control, AI behavior, and turret mechanics in Unreal Engine. The tank is fully controlled via C++ scripts, handling movement and interactions. Additionally, the turret system was also implemented in C++, allowing it to autonomously detect and shoot at enemies, specifically targeting the player’s tank. The simple enemy AI was designed using Behavior Trees (BT). In this implementation, the enemy approaches the player, deploys a turret, and then moves away to restock turrets before returning to engage again.
