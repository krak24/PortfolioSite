Project Description

This is my most extensive project to date, developed over six months as part of a game jam. I took on the roles of both programmer and game designer while also leading a team of three artists, a sound designer, and a writer. My responsibilities included designing and implementing minigames, developing a custom command-based engine in Unity for the visual novel – drawing inspiration from Ren’Py. This project also serves as the foundation for my engineering thesis. Once the approval process is completed by the university, I will provide a link to the final version in the Link section below. The development process was an intense grind, requiring a significant amount of work and learning. From a technical perspective, I gained valuable experience in text architecture, object-oriented programming, and data-driven design. Over the course of six months, I wrote more than 600,000 characters of code in Visual Studio, making it one of the most time-consuming and complex projects I have worked on.
