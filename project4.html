Project Description

As part of my university coursework, I developed multiple AI-driven projects where AI programming played a key role. This particular project integrates two AI systems—Utility AI and Behavior Trees—within a single environment. A wolf, controlled by Utility AI, manages its needs (hunger, thirst, and sleep), while a cat follows a Behavior Tree system. The primary goal was to create a well-structured and readable Blueprint-based AI system.
